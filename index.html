IoT-Based Air Exchanger system for indoor air quality management. Built with ESP32, sensors (CO₂, PM2.5, VOC, temp, humidity), and Arduino IoT Cloud. Features automated fan control, SD card logging, LCD display, and remote monitoring for computer labs.

The project introduces an audio-responsive RGB lamp that turns on in response to sound. Designed as a recreational lighting solution, enhancing the mood of the environment, making the act of turning on a lamp an enjoyable interaction.
